Core Features

Built for local video preservation work.

The app focuses on getting public video evidence out of volatile web pages and into local files with useful context.

Detect and download

Start from a public source URL, detect available media, and preserve video files into a selected local evidence folder.

Clip ranges

Capture the relevant segment instead of storing entire long videos when only a specific moment matters.

Live recording

Record a selected screen region for live streams, disappearing playback, or sources that cannot be downloaded directly.

Audio handling

Choose audio sources, preserve system audio where configured, and control volume for cleaner local recordings.

Queue and thumbnails

Track active, completed, paused, failed, and cancelled jobs with thumbnails and quick reveal actions.

Preservation logs

Keep job history, source URLs, output paths, capture times, and status logs close to the saved video files.

Workflow

From public video source to local evidence file.

The product is intentionally narrow: find the media, preserve the clip, keep the local file and log clear.

01 Choose output folder

Set the destination for downloaded videos, clips, recordings, thumbnails, and logs.

02 Open the source

Paste the public URL or prepare the live playback area for recording.

03 Preserve the media

Download a video, capture a clip range, or record the selected screen region.

04 Review the file

Use the queue, thumbnails, reveal actions, and job log to verify what was saved.

Standalone By Design

Use Video Downloader for acquisition and SnapCase Suite for the case package.

Keeping the tools separate lets the downloader stay fast and focused while the suite remains centered on case evidence, reports, OCR, targets, and cross-case intelligence.
